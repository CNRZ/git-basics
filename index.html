Engineering Services

Chemical engineers provide unique skills to tackle common and often complex industry probelms. Time Management

People spend significant amounts of time doing tedious work and chores effectively limiting the time they can spend doing the things they love. The most effective strategies and tools I have found to save time are organizational planning, polyphasic sleeping, and educational investment in the short-long-run.

Sustainability

Due to limited resources it is socially and spiritually convenient to keep others in mind when making a purchase. Everything you buy affects someone in one way or another, for good or for bad. One way of reducing impact is minimization, ironically this also paves the path toward abundant and healthy living.

Often we cannot perform at our peak because our environment is unsuatable. Let's change that!
